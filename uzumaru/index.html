Uzumaru

Uzumaru combines the Japanese words "uzumaki" (spiral) and "maru" (circle/complete) into an elegant light sculpture that embodies mathematical harmony found in nature.

The centerpiece features a 256 LED matrix arranged in a Fibonacci floral pattern, mimicking a sunflower's natural geometry. All plastic components are custom 3D printed, available in black or white variations, and in tall or short models.

Features:

  • Powered by USB-C for easy setup anywhere
  • Controlled by a single color-backlit encoder knob for intuitive interaction
  • 70+ color palettes and 20 distinct light patterns
  • Custom software running on an ESP32 microcontroller
  • Open-source firmware at github.com/costyn/Splendida2024
  • Adjust brightness, animation speed, and playlist functions with the control knob
